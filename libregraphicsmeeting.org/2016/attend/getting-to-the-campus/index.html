Getting to the campus

By tube:

The Harrow Campus is situated near to the following underground stations:

Northwick Park — Metropolitan line

  • Turn left after the gates and exit the station. Don’t forget to tap out.
  • You should see the entry of the campus (through the gate).
  • Follow the signage from there on.

Kenton — Bakerloo line

  • Exit the station to the left, onto Kenton Road
  • Take the second street on the left, onto Rushout Avenue
  • Turn right at the end of Rushout Avenue, onto Northwick Avenue
  • Turn left and to the underground station Nortwick park and pass through the tunnel.
  • You should see the entry of the campus (through the gate).
  • Follow the signage from there on.

By train:

The Harrow Campus is situated near to the Kenton rail station:

  • Exit the station to the left, onto Kenton Road
  • Take the second street on the left, onto Rushout Avenue
  • Turn right at the end of Rushout Avenue, onto Northwick Avenue
  • Turn left and to the underground station Nortwick park and pass through the tunnel.
  • You should see the entry of the campus (through the gate).
  • Follow the signage from there on.

By bus:

The Harrow Campus is situated near to the following bus routes:

Watford Road — 114, 140, 129

By bike:

The Harrow Campus is accessible by bike and there are bike docks on site.

By car:

The Harrow Campus has a car park which operates a pay and display ticket facility.

For info on accessibility and directions please see link here.

To plan your journey please go to Getting around London page.
