Helping out

LGM is organised by volunteers, this year supported by the team of Medialab Prado. You can help making the meeting a success in various ways:

  • Help with communicating LGM, press-releases, spreading the word
  • Donate to the crowdfunding-campaign (coming soon)
  • Sponsor the meeting
  • Be available on-site to help with practical tasks
  • Propose a presentation (coming soon)
  • Participate!


To let us know how you’d like to contribute, please sign up to the LGM-mailinglist: http://lists.freedesktop.org/mailman/listinfo/libre-graphics-meeting or find us at #LGM on irc.freenode.net.
