Hotels in Montréal

If you wish to stay outside the campus, no problem! Montreal has lots of hotels of all kinds and at all prices. Hôtel Terrasse Royale is located at walkable distance from École Polytechnique. Otherwise we can suggest that you look for hotels near the following métro stations as to save commute time to LGM: Guy-Concordia, Place-des-Arts, Berri-UQAM, Sherbrooke, Mont-Royal, Laurier.

It’s also easy to rent an apartment or find a bed & breakfast or to do couch surfing !
