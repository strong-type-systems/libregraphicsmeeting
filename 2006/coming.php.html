Getting there

The Libre Graphics Meeting is on the university campus at La Doua, in Villeurbane, near Lyon.

Getting there by plane

Lyon's Airport is called Lyon Saint Exupry (LYS). From there, you can reach the city center by the shuttlebus called " Satobus " which will take you to Perrache station. Then take the T1 tramway to the campus. The nearest stop is "Gaston Berger" (see map above).

Getting there by train

There are two main train station in Lyon: Perrache and La Part-Dieu. From both of them, you can take the T1 tramway to go to the campus.
