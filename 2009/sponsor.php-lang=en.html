Platinum sponsors

The community has given more than $12,000 for last year's LGM in Wroclaw. The pledgie for this year's meeting is still running and you can make your donation at http://pledgie.com/campaigns/2926 .

Last year, we raised over $12,000 in community donations for the Libre Graphics Meeting. This year, we are again running a fundraising campaign to allow us to cover the costs of the conference.

Especially this year, with sponsorship budgets being cut across the board, your support is essential to allow us to put on the conference, and ensure that we have a critical mass of involved developers present. The vast majority of our budget is spent subsidising the travel of the developers who create software such as GIMP, Scribus, Inkscape and other free software creative projects.


Please help support the Libre Graphics Meeting and the projects that participate in it – every little helps. Thank you!
